About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research (IEOR) at the University of California, Berkeley, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Chiwei Yan. Previously, I received my M.S. in Data Science in the University of Michigan. My research focuses on improving transportation and logistics systems using techniques of optimization and stochastic modeling.
